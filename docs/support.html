Common questions

How do I restore my subscription?
Settings → Restore Purchases in the app.

How do I cancel?
iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Mahj Trainer.

Does the app include the official card?
No. Mahj Trainer teaches the category system with original practice hands, so your skills work with any year's card. For official hands and values, get the current NMJL card.
